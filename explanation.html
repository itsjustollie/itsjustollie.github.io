What is Bendy?
Home Story Explanation

Explanation

The way Bendy works is that there are five chapters that you unlock in order. The way it works is each chapter continues the story each having a new way to play. Chapter one is a tutorial level which shows you how to pick up items, run and jump (you never use jump for some reason) and chapter two is more of a puzzle level with levers and doors.


Chapter three is a fetch quest type of level which is a level where you do tasks and move on to the next one. Then the next chapter which is the fourth one is a chapter that starts a boss fight which are villians which are stronger than normal and have a one on one fight. After all that you have chapter five which is a chapter with puzzle, enemies, story and a boss fight with the Ink Demon to finish.
