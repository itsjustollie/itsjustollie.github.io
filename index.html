What is bendy?

Hi and welcome to my explanation on Bendy and the Ink Machine which is a horror game. If you have seen my explanation of FNAF then you would know I am going to do a strong explanation of the story, characters and overall everything.

Story

Bendy and the Ink Machine is a horror game in the 1960's that is based on cartoons. There's lots of lore and secrets. There's lots of ink and cutouts all over the place.


Villain

There's one antagonist and it's Bendy himself he is known as the Ink Demon and some of his powers are teleporting, one hit killing and hears everything.
